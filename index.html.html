Mt. Yangbew is one of the localsâ€™ favorite hiking, camping or sunrise/sunset viewing near Baguio. It is also known as Mt. Jumbo or Mt. Jambo because it used to be the place for the National Scout Jamboree of the Boy Scouts of America when the Americans were in the region decades ago.

Locals also refer to it as the little Mt. Pulag because of the lack of trees at the summit which is reminiscent of Pulag.

This is the amazing sunrise spot of La Trinidad. It has rolling grass that changes colors with seasons and offers a majestic view of the La Trinidad valley and even surounding towns.

Mt.Yangbew is amoung the mountain ranges surrounding La Trinidad locatesd in Tawang. The hike from the jumpinf-off point to the summit is around 30 minutes.
